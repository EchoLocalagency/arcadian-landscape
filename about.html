About Arcadian Landscape

Meet the Founder

Evan Weisman

Evan's path to landscape design started in an unexpected place -- a graphic design program at Cal State. But a summer job changed everything. Working outdoors with plants and soil, he discovered a calling that combined his artistic instincts with a deep respect for the natural world.

He went on to complete the CSU Landscape Design program, where he built the technical foundation in horticulture, irrigation, grading, and construction that backs every project today. With over 10 years of hands-on experience across San Diego County, Evan brings a rare combination of creative vision and construction expertise to every landscape he touches.

His specialties include sustainable horticulture, native and drought-tolerant plantings, and succulent gardens designed specifically for San Diego's coastal climate.

Philosophy

Our Design Philosophy

Evan views each landscape as an artistic composition. Every project starts with the big picture -- how the space flows, how the eye moves, how seasons will shift the palette. He plans for continuity by strategically selecting colors, textures, heights, sizes, and blooming times that create a living tapestry rather than a static arrangement.

"I take ownership of every landscape I manage, so I treat it like it's my own."

-- Evan Weisman, Founder

Our Process

How We Work

From initial concept to finished landscape, we guide you through a proven three-step process that keeps every project on track and on budget.

Step 1

Free Consultation

We meet you on-site to walk the property, discuss your vision, and assess the space. This is where we learn how you use your yard, what you love, and what is not working. No pressure, no obligation -- just a real conversation about what is possible.

Step 2

Design Development

We create a preliminary design tailored to your property and lifestyle. This includes detailed plant selections, material choices, grading plans, and spatial layouts. You will see exactly what your landscape will look like before a single shovel hits the ground.

Step 3

Expert Construction

Our licensed crew builds your landscape to spec, managing every detail from hardscape and irrigation to planting and finish work. Evan oversees the entire project personally, so nothing gets lost in translation between the design and the build.
