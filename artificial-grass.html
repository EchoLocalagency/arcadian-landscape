Artificial Grass Installation in San Diego

Green Without the Water

A Perfect Lawn, Every Day of the Year

Natural grass in San Diego is a losing battle. It needs constant water in a region where water is scarce and expensive. It needs mowing, edging, fertilizing, aerating, and overseeding -- week after week, month after month. It goes brown in summer drought restrictions and muddy in winter rains. Artificial grass eliminates every one of these problems. You get a lawn that looks perfect year-round with almost zero maintenance and zero water.

At Arcadian Landscape, we install artificial grass that looks and feels like the real thing. Evan Weisman selects premium turf products with multi-toned blades, natural thatch layers, and realistic textures that fool the eye from any distance. We do not just roll out turf on top of dirt -- every installation starts with proper excavation, compacted base material, and drainage engineering that ensures the surface stays flat, drains perfectly, and performs for 15 to 25 years.

Whether you want to replace a water-hungry front lawn, build a backyard putting green, create a pet-friendly play area, or outfit a commercial property, we design and install artificial grass systems that deliver real value in San Diego's climate.

What We Install

Artificial Grass Applications

From front lawns to putting greens, every installation is engineered for its specific use and built to last.

Residential Lawns

The front lawn is where most homeowners start. It is the most visible part of your property, it consumes the most water, and it takes the most maintenance time. Replacing natural turf with artificial grass transforms your curb appeal overnight. No more brown patches in summer. No more muddy spots after rain. No more Saturday mornings behind a mower. Just a clean, green, perfectly manicured lawn that looks the same in January as it does in July.

We install residential lawns with a minimum 4-inch compacted aggregate base, permeable weed barrier, and premium turf secured with galvanized nails and seamed with outdoor adhesive. The edges are finished with bender board or aluminum edging that keeps the perimeter clean and prevents base material from migrating. Every lawn includes proper drainage pitch so water runs through the turf and off the base rather than pooling on the surface.

Putting Greens

A backyard putting green is the ultimate luxury for San Diego golfers. Our putting greens use specialized short-pile nylon turf that rolls true and holds approach shots like a real green. We build contours, breaks, and elevation changes into the base so the green plays like a real course -- not a flat carpet. Fringe turf around the edges adds a realistic transition and lets you practice chip shots and pitches.

Every putting green we build starts with a detailed grade plan. We shape the sub-base with decomposed granite to create the undulations and breaks you want, then compact and fine-tune until the surface rolls exactly right. Cup placements are set in reinforced sleeves so you can move the holes for variety. The result is a practice facility in your backyard that plays consistently day after day, regardless of weather or season.

Pet-Friendly Turf

Dogs and natural grass do not mix. Urine burns create dead spots. Digging destroys root systems. Muddy paws track dirt inside. Fleas and ticks thrive in organic turf. Pet-specific artificial grass solves every one of these issues. The turf is non-toxic, flea-resistant, and engineered with antimicrobial infill that neutralizes odor-causing bacteria.

Our pet turf installations use enhanced drainage systems that handle liquids at rates exceeding 30 inches per hour -- far faster than any natural lawn. Solid waste picks up cleanly from the surface without leaving residue. We install a slightly thicker base with additional drainage rock to ensure the sub-base stays dry and odor-free. For multi-dog households, we recommend a dedicated pet area with easy-rinse access, keeping the rest of your landscape pristine.

Play Areas

Artificial grass creates a clean, cushioned, and allergen-free surface for children's play areas. No grass stains on clothes. No mud tracked inside. No pesticides or fertilizers that kids and toddlers contact during play. The surface provides consistent traction for running and playing, and the padded underlayment we install absorbs impact from falls -- meeting ASTM safety standards for playground surfacing.

We install play area turf with a shock-absorbing pad beneath the turf that provides critical fall height protection. The turf itself is soft, durable, and designed to withstand heavy foot traffic without matting down. For families with children, a turf play area is one of the most practical backyard investments you can make -- it gets used daily, requires virtually no maintenance, and stays safe and clean year-round.

Commercial Turf

Commercial properties face the same water and maintenance challenges as residential ones, but at a larger scale. Artificial grass eliminates mowing crews, irrigation repairs, fertilizer applications, and the brown-lawn embarrassment that comes with San Diego's drought restrictions. For commercial landscapes, HOA common areas, and multi-family properties, the maintenance savings alone justify the investment within a few years.

We install commercial turf with heavy-duty backing and higher-density fiber counts that withstand the foot traffic of shared spaces. The base system is engineered for the heavier loads of commercial use, with deeper aggregate and reinforced edging. We work with property managers, HOAs, and business owners to design installations that improve curb appeal, reduce operating costs, and eliminate the liability of uneven, poorly maintained natural turf.

Why Artificial Grass

The San Diego Case for Synthetic Turf

Water Savings That Add Up

A 1,000-square-foot natural lawn in San Diego uses approximately 44,000 gallons of water per year. At current water rates, that is $300 to $500 annually just for the lawn. Over 15 years -- the minimum lifespan of quality artificial grass -- that is $4,500 to $7,500 in water savings alone. Add in the elimination of mowing, fertilizing, aerating, and pest control, and the total savings over the life of the turf typically exceeds the installation cost. Artificial grass is not just an expense -- it is an investment that pays for itself.

Always Green, Always Ready

San Diego's climate means outdoor living happens year-round. Your lawn should be ready for it. Artificial grass does not go dormant in winter, does not burn out in summer drought, and does not turn to mud in the occasional rain. It looks the same every single day. When guests arrive for a barbecue, when potential buyers drive by your home, when your kids run out the back door after school -- the lawn is perfect. That consistency has a real value that is hard to quantify but impossible to ignore.

Installation ROI

Beyond water and maintenance savings, artificial grass increases property value. Real estate agents in San Diego consistently report that homes with well-installed artificial grass attract more buyer interest, especially in drought-conscious markets. The investment pays back in monthly savings, lifestyle improvement, and resale value. For rental properties and vacation homes, it eliminates the need for landscape maintenance entirely -- a significant cost and management headache for remote property owners.

How We Work

Our Installation Process

From site measurement to the final infill brush, here is how we install artificial grass that lasts.

Site Visit & Measurement

Evan visits your property to measure the area, assess soil conditions and drainage, check for underground utilities, and discuss your goals. We help you select the right turf product -- blade height, color, density, and backing -- based on how the area will be used. This visit is free and carries no obligation.

Excavation & Base Prep

We remove existing turf, soil, and organic material to a depth of 3 to 4 inches. A weed barrier goes down first, followed by class II road base aggregate that is graded for drainage and compacted with a plate compactor until it is rock solid. The base is the most critical part of the installation -- skip it or rush it and the turf will settle, wrinkle, and drain poorly.

Turf Installation

We roll out the turf, trim to fit, and seam any joints with outdoor-rated adhesive and seaming tape. The turf is secured around the perimeter with galvanized nails every 4 to 6 inches and finished with bender board or aluminum edging. Every cut is precise, every seam is invisible, and every edge is clean.

Infill & Final Brushing

We spread silica sand or antimicrobial infill evenly across the surface and brush it into the turf fibers with a power broom. Infill weighs the turf down, supports the blades in an upright position, and provides ballast that keeps the surface stable in wind. The final power-brush lifts every fiber for a natural, freshly-mowed look. We walk you through care tips and hand over your new, maintenance-free lawn.
