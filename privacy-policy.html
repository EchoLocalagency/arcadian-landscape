Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

Arcadian Landscape ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website arcadianlandscape.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website or contact us through our forms.

Information We Collect

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you use our contact form or request a consultation. This includes:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Message content describing your project or inquiry

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as financial data, Social Security numbers, or health information through this website.

How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or consultation request
  • Follow up about your landscape project
  • Provide estimates or proposals you have requested

We do not use your information for automated marketing campaigns or unsolicited communications.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies for basic analytics purposes to help us understand how visitors use the site. These cookies collect anonymous data such as pages visited, time on site, and referring sources. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics cookies.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data with marketing companies or data brokers. Your information stays with Arcadian Landscape and is used only for the purposes described above.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. California residents may have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
