Our Service Areas in San Diego County

Premium Landscape Design from La Jolla to Carlsbad

Based in Cardiff-by-the-Sea, Arcadian Landscape serves homeowners throughout San Diego's coastal and North County communities. Owner Evan Weisman and his team bring over a decade of experience to every project, designing and building outdoor spaces that reflect the unique character of each neighborhood. From oceanfront estates to hillside retreats, we understand the local climate, soil conditions, and architectural styles that make each area distinct.

La Jolla

La Jolla's dramatic coastal setting demands landscape designs that complement ocean views while standing up to salt air and wind exposure. Arcadian Landscape has completed numerous projects in La Jolla's Bird Rock, Windansea, and Mount Soledad neighborhoods. We specialize in creating drought-tolerant coastal gardens, stone hardscape that blends with the natural bluffs, and outdoor living spaces designed to maximize those Pacific panoramas.

La Jolla Landscaping

Encinitas

Encinitas homeowners value a relaxed, organic aesthetic that mirrors the community's surf culture and horticultural heritage. From Leucadia's eclectic bungalows to the estates of Olivenhain, Arcadian designs landscapes that feel natural and lived-in. We work with native plantings, natural stone, and sustainable irrigation systems that honor the city's deep connection to its botanical roots, including the famed San Diego Botanic Garden just up the road.

Encinitas Landscaping

Carlsbad

Carlsbad's master-planned communities and established neighborhoods offer a wide range of landscape opportunities. Whether it is a contemporary backyard renovation in Bressi Ranch or a full front-yard transformation in the Village, Arcadian brings design precision and construction quality. We build custom patios, fire features, and planting schemes that work within HOA guidelines while still reflecting your personal style.

Carlsbad Landscaping

Del Mar

Del Mar properties sit among some of San Diego's most coveted real estate, and the landscaping should match. Arcadian Landscape designs refined outdoor environments for Del Mar homeowners who expect nothing less than exceptional craftsmanship. We build elegant hardscape entries, water features that create a sense of calm, and lush drought-tolerant plantings that look striking year-round without wasting water.

Del Mar Landscaping

Rancho Santa Fe

Rancho Santa Fe's sprawling estates and equestrian properties call for landscape designs on a grand scale. Arcadian has designed and built multi-phase projects throughout The Covenant, Fairbanks Ranch, and Cielo. Our work in Rancho Santa Fe includes custom stone walls, resort-style pool surrounds, mature tree installations, and irrigation systems engineered for large acreage properties that balance beauty with water efficiency.

Rancho Santa Fe Landscaping

Carmel Valley

Carmel Valley's family-friendly neighborhoods feature newer homes with backyards that are ready for thoughtful upgrades. Arcadian Landscape helps Carmel Valley homeowners turn standard builder yards into functional outdoor living spaces with paver patios, BBQ islands, artificial turf play areas, and low-maintenance planting. We design with families in mind, creating spaces that work for entertaining, play, and everyday life.

Carmel Valley Landscaping

Solana Beach

Solana Beach's compact lots and coastal proximity make every square foot count. Arcadian Landscape specializes in maximizing smaller outdoor spaces with smart design -- multi-level patios, vertical gardens, and lighting that extends usable hours into the evening. We work with Solana Beach homeowners who want outdoor areas that feel larger than they are, using clean lines, strategic plantings, and quality hardscape materials.

Solana Beach Landscaping

Cardiff-by-the-Sea

Cardiff is home -- it is where Arcadian Landscape is based and where Evan Weisman got his start. We know Cardiff's microclimates, soil types, and neighborhood character better than anyone. From the homes along San Elijo to the properties above Restaurant Row, we have built dozens of projects in our own community. Cardiff homeowners trust Arcadian because we are their neighbors, and our reputation here speaks for itself.

Cardiff Landscaping
